Grilled Cheese

Bread, butter, and cheese. What's not to love?

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Place pan on stove, use medium to medium-low heat
  2. Prepare the bread by spreading butter onto one side of each bread slice
  3. Place one slice of bread butter-side down
  4. Put cheese on top of the slice of bread
  5. Place the last slice of bread butter-side up
  6. Once golden brown, flip
  7. Plate up once second side is browned
